About

My name is Henry Widd, I am an independent software consultant who has been developing software for over 15 years, mostly in the financial and aerospace domains. See my LinkedIn profile for more info.

Some notable examples of my open source work include:

  • Author of the first cross-platform Clojure(Script) date/time library cljc.java-time (and its companion time-literals)
  • Contributior to Clojurescript (the compiler)
  • Co-author of tick
  • stats-atom, a drop-in replacement for clojure.core/atom which maintains update stats
